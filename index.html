About Us

Mauricugo International Limited is a registered Nigerian-based trading company. We are born out of the drive to create, source, import, and export quality products such as crops, pharmaceuticals, and cosmetics for small and large business setups.

We do not only focus on creating availability but also pay keen attention to their marketing and distribution. With the aid of our global trading expertise, we can reach out to customers around the world.

With great interest to create solutions to your supply queries, we employ experts with in-depth knowledge and great business insights to provide independent expert advisory services. As a team, we are a trading company ready to fuel your business growth.

Our Services

Import and Export

We handle all import and export needs of individuals and organizations. We import a wide range of products such as automobiles, machinery, electrical appliances and much more. Within Nigeria, we supply major food crops like soybeans, rice, maize to local crop dealers and help manufacturers export their produce.

Distribution and Marketing

We serve as a trading agency to Manufacturers who wish to expand their market in Nigeria and other African countries. We also offer home-based marketing services to individuals and organizations who want to increase their sales in Nigeria.

Logistics

We provide efficient and reliable transportation services to individuals and organizations. We help you meet your customer requirements in a timely and cost-effective manner.

Business Registration

We provide business and trademark registration for companies, schools, clubs, Religious centers, associations, and NGOs.
